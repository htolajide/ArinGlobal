About Us

Building Nigeria's future with quality materials and exceptional service

Your Trusted Building Materials Partner Since Day One

Arinola Multipurpose Global Limited is a leading dealer in high-quality building materials, proudly serving contractors, builders, and homeowners across Oyo State and beyond.

We specialize in supplying a comprehensive range of building materials — from various types of boards and modern ceiling solutions to durable roofing sheets and essential construction accessories — all at competitive prices.

Our commitment is simple: quality products, honest pricing, reliable service, and timely delivery. Whether you're building from scratch, renovating, or working on a large-scale construction project, we have everything you need under one roof.
